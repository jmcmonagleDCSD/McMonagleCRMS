Iron Ore

Iron ore is the most used ore out of the three by far. Iron ore is found in Iron Nods. For a MK 1 Miner, an impure node will provide 30pm, a normal node will provide 60pm, and a pure node will provide 120pm.







Copper Ore

Copper ore is used in all eletronic related items. Copper ore is found in Copper Nods. For a MK 1 Miner, an impure node will provide 30pm, a normal node will provide 60pm, and a pure node will provide 120pm.







Limestone

Limestone is used in the construction of concrete. Limestone can be found in Limestone Nods. For a MK 1 Miner, an impure node will provide 30pm, a normal node will provide 60pm, and a pure node will provide 120pm.







Coal

Coal is used to make steel ingots and power a coal generator. For a MK 1 Miner, an impure node will provide 30pm, a normal node will provide 60pm, and a pure node will provide 120pm.







Leaves

Leaves are collected by picking them off the ground, or cutting down trees with the chainsaw in order to collect them. They can be crafted into biomass which can be used to fuel your biomass burners.







Wood

Wood is a rarer to find than leaves, but they are more efficent in the creation of biomass. You can mainly find them while cutting down trees.







Flower Petals

Flower petals are the most rare of the three natural plants found in the game. They are used to make color cartriges and to power biomass burners. There is no need to craft biomass out of them, they are most efficent when just put into the biomass burner themselves. They are found throuhgout and can be picked up from the ground.

Iron Ingot

Copper Ingot

Steel Ingot

Iron Plates

Iron plates are pretty important in the early game. They are used for the belt MK1 construction and in the construction of other machines and storage. In a single constructor at 100% efficency, it will make 20 iron plates pm with 30 ingots pm.







Iron Rods

Iron rods are used all around in construction. Mainly in the creation of other resources or machines. In a single constructior at 100% efficency, it will make 10 iron rods pm with 15 ingots pm.







Screws

Screws are mainly used in the construction of reinforced iron plates and rotors. They are also used throughout the rest of the playthrough in recipes for resources. In a single constructior at 100% efficency, it will make 40 screws pm with 10 iron rods pm.







Reinforced Iron Plates

Reinforced iron plates are used for making constructiors, assemblers, etc. They are also used in the construction of the MK2 conveyer belts. In a single assembler at 100% efficency, it will make 5 reinforced iron plates with 40 screws and 30 iron plates pm.







Rotors

Rotors are used in the construction of assemblers and other machines and resources. One of the main resources they are used in is motors. In an assembler at 100% efficency, it will make 4 rotors with 100 screws and 20 iron rods pm.







Modular Frames

Modular frames are used in the construction of some important resources. In an assembler at 100% efficency, it will make 2 modular frames with 12 iron rods and 3 reinforced iron plates pm.







Steel Beams

Steel beams are mainly used in the construction of MK3 belts that move 270 item pm. In a constructor at 100% efficency, it will make 15 beams with 60 steel ingots pm.







Steel Pipes

Steel pipes are used here and there and are not for one specific thing. In a constructor at 100% efficency, it will make 20pm with 30 steel ingots pm.







Encased Industrial Beams

Enchased industrial beams are mainly used to create the MK4 belts that move 480 items pm. In a assembler at 100% efficency, it will make 6 with 24 steel ingots and 30 concrete pm.







Wire

Wire is used for the construction of cable. Wire is also used here and there in certain places. In a constructor at 100% efficency, it will make 30 wire with 15 copper ingots pm.







Cable

Cable is used for powerlines and connecting machines to power poles which connnect to your power plant. In a constructor at 100% efficency, it will make 30 cable with 60 wire pm.







Concrete

Concrete is used in mainly the construction of foundations and walls for your factory. They are also used in certain recipes and machines. In a constructor at 100% efficency, it will make 15 concrete with 45 limestone pm.







Biomass

Biomass is used to power your biomass burners. They are decently efficent for this purpose. You can craft them using two different items, leaves, and wood. Both in a constructor at 100% efficency, leaves will make 5 biomass with 10 leaves, and wood will make 20 biomass with 4 wood.

Miner MK1

The miner MK1 is placed on top of resource nodes to extract the ore from them. For the MK1 miner, a impure node will produce 30 ore pm, a normal node will produce 60 ore pm, and a pure node will produce 120 ore pm.







Constructor

These will become your best friend. They are used in almost every single factory and take in one input of item and porduces one output of an item.







Assembler

Assemblers are used for more advanced construction of resources. They take in two items and produce one item.







Smelter

The smelter takes in a ore input and makes the ingot output. Only takes in one input and one output.







Foundry

Foundry also takes in ores and creates a ingot. Takes in two inputs and produces one output.







Conveyor Belt MK1

These are constructed using iron plates and transport up to 60 items pm.







Conveyor Belt MK2

These are constructed using reinforced iron plates and transport up to 120 items pm.







Conveyor Splitter

This splits evenly one conveyor belt into up to three seperate belts.







Conveyor Merger

This merges up to three belts onto one single belt.







Conveyor Pole

This supports the conveyor so it is not on the ground.







Power Pole MK1

This can make up to four connections using cable. They connect your machines to your power grid so they can work properly.







Biomass Burner

These are used at the begining of the game to power your buildings. They are manually loaded with biomass and provide 30 MW of power. Keep in mind that the biomass burners attatched to the HUB only produce 20 MW of power.







Coal Generator

The coal generator is an automatic way to produce power. One single generator at 100% efficency will produce 75 MW of power with 15 coal pm and 45 water pm.







Factory Foundation

These are used to organize and more accuratly place machines and conveyor belts. When you place someting on a foundation, it will snap to a grid making it eaiser to allign belts and machines. Where if you were to place a machine or belt on the ground it would be placed freely.







Water Extractor

The water extractor extracts water fron ponds, rivers, lakes, and oceans. A water extractor with no overclocking will provide 120 water pm.







Pipeline Mk1

The pipeline MK1 is the first of two varients of the pipeline. A single pipe can transport up to 300 fluid pm.







Pipeline Junction

The pipeline junction is like the splitter and merger in one. It automaticly either splits or merges liquids based of the direction your pumps and flow is going. Can make up to four connections. Keep in mind that you can't combine two different liquids onto a single pipe.







Pump MK1

The pump mk1 is placed on pipelines and pumps liquid 20 meters in a single direction. They are used to move liquids uphill or in a certain direction. Liquid will not go up if there is no pump to push up the liquid, so make sure you place them here and there to make sure your liquid doesn't get stuck in your pipes.







Small Fluid Buffer

The small fluid buffer hold liquid inside of it. It has an input and output where you can attact your pipelines. The small fluid buffer can hold 400 of any liquid.

Refinery

From now on with alternate recipes and everything, you will use this machine more than any othe one. So make sure you understand it. It takes in two inputs, one solid matter and the other liquid. It also outputs the same, one solid matter belt and a pipe.







Manufacturer

The manufactuer is used to make complex resources. Get used to this one aswell because all the future advanced resources are made in here. It takes in 3 or 4 inputs to make 1 output.







Miner MK2

The miner MK2 is also amazing. It doubles the amount of ore pm you would get from a MK1 miner. For all the nodes at 100% efficency, an impure will provide 60 ore pm, a normal node will provide 120 ore pm, and a pure node will provide 240 ore pm.







Fuel Generator

The fuel generator takes in 12.5 fuel pm to make 150 MW of power for your power grid. I reccomend using alternate recipes to turn oil into heavy oil residue and then make fuel out of that to power your grid.







Packager

The packager is used to package liquids into containers for easy transport or as fuel for your jetpack or vehicles. It takes containers made from plastic and the liquid you want to package and combines them. You can package any liquid you want in the entire game.







Belt MK4

The belt MK4 is made from encased industrial beams and can transport up to 480 items pm. You shouldn't really be using these for long, I would reccomend only using them to create your bauxite factory so you can get the MK5 belts faster.







Belt Mk5

The belt MK5 is the best and last versions of the conveyor belts. They are made from aluminum sheets and can transport up to 780 items pm.







Oil Extractor

The oil extractor is placed ontop of oil nodes. They will collect oil just like a miner, but the numbers are different. For an impure node it will produce 60 oil pm, a normal node will produce 120pm, and a pure node will produce 240 pm.







Smart Splitter

The smart splitter is a interesting version of the splitter. You can decide where to split items and set a priority line and if that line backs up, you can make the rest of the items go a different way. Although, as soon as that priority line starts moving again, that overflow line won't get any material.







MAM

The MAM is short for molecular analysis machine, meaning it will analyze the data of whatever you put into it. The MAM is used to reaserach unknown minerals such as caterium or quartz, alien organizms, alien plants, and power slugs. I reccomend you start using the MAM as early as possible so you have all the branchs unlocked for when you need them later down the line.







Trains

Trains are very useful and I greatly reccomend you understand them. The trains themselves are easy to understand, they can be autopioleted to drive to other trainstations or manualy driven. If two seperate trains or a train derails you will be notified. A trains top speed is 120 KM per hour. The fuel is your power and the mimimum power it uses is 25 MW. It takes more power when it has to speed back up, rather that be braking before a turn then gasing out of it or heading uphill. You can have two trains attaced to eachother, and multiple trains on a single track.







Freight Car

The freight car is attaced behind a train or another freight car. They can hold items that can be loaded and unloaded at freight platforms. I reccomend you have a ratio of 4 freight cars for every train in order to keep that max speed of 120 KM per hour.







Train Station

The train station is where your trains will stop and go. They can be named and once placed, can be found in the train's time table to make that train go to it.







Freight Platform

The freight platform is where you will either load or unload your items from your freight cars. Thay are attached to the back of trainstations and 1 freight platform will load/unload 1 freight car.







Block Signal

This is where things start to get complex with trains. Only use block and path signals if you plan to have multiple trains on a single track. The block signal is placed on an end segment of a railway. What it does is seperate the track into different "blocks" What they do is say there is a train in the block ahead. If a train aproaches the block before the train before the train ahead exits the block, it will stop until that train leaves the block. This prevents train collisions. I reccomend placing one blocksignal for 2 full railway tracks.







Path Signals

Path signals are more complex. They are placed on the enterances to junctions. Junctions are like stoplights but for trains. What it does is when the train aproaches, it will check if the path it is going is clear. If it is, the train will go right on through. If the train can't go through, it will wait at the begining of the junction. The cool think about this is it will let two trains go into the junction at once if their paths don't intersect. An example would be two trains going straight but on oposite sides. If you just used block signals one train would stop because there is a train in that block, but path signals allow trains to move if thier path is clear.
