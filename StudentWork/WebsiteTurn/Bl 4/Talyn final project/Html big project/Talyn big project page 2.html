Tom Brady: his life story

From wikimediacommons: By Todd Shoemake

Tom Brady was born on August 3, 1977, in San Mateo, California, United States. He regularly attended 49ers games to watch his idol Joe Montana. He was raised as a catholic and attended many football camps in his early years. Tom Brady started his quarterback career as a backup JV quarterback at his high school in San Mateo, California. after that he started quarterback at the University of Michigan and did pretty well. when He got drafted he was upset because he was the last quarterback picked in the draft as an extra. When the starter Drew bledsoe got hurt he finally got his chance to show what he was really made of. After showing everybody who he was Bill belchick (the head coach) decided to keep him in. After winning 6 super bowls with the patriots and one with the buccaneers Tom Brady is arguably the best player the sport has ever seen.
