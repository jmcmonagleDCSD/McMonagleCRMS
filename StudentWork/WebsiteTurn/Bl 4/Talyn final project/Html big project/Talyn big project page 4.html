Facts

His story

Peyton Manning was born on March 24, 1976, in New Orleans, Louisiana. Peyton Manning was highly regarded as a high-school player. During his senior season He received a national Player of the Year award. He attended the University of Tennessee, where he was the starting quarterback for four years. he got drafted by the colts in 1998 and had amazing seasons with them. A couple years later he transferred to the Denver Broncos and some reporters said (He picked up right where he left off.) He led the broncos too four super bowl appearances and two super bowl wins. He retired from professional football in March 2016, with career totals of 71,940 passing yards and 539 touchdowns. Manning was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2021.
