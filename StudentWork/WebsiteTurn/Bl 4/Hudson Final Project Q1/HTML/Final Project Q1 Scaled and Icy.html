Out of Trench

Tyler was now put to work by the bishops to spread propaganda across the world.
Tyler worked on a submarine underwater, performing to a large crowd of unsuspecting people.
Then a pet of the bishops, Trash.
An underwater dragon roaming the sea seized by Keons, one of the Niners.
Also known as a bishop.
Seizing is an art that the bishops use which means they can control a dead animal, or person.
Although, you can see when something is seized their eyes turn yellow.
Well, Keons seizing Trash crashed him into the submarine making the entire thing flood.
Tyler washed up on an island named Voldsoy and wandered.
Now stranded Tyler has no idea what to do now that he escaped from Dema.
However he starts seeing something on the island, something moving, almost like it is hiding.
Then Tyler finds this cave, and a torch.
He starts going in to find dozens of little furry creatures.
These things are called Neds, they are little furry antlered creatures that have big black eyes.
They are showing Tyler around the cave and there are more and more of them hiding all throughout the cave.
Then one of them gives Tyler its antlers.
At the same time Keons was killed because he had betrayed the bishops, by using Trash to help Tyler escape.
Tyler put the antlers up to his head and the power of the antlers allowed him to seize Keons.
He did a dance and destroyed lights in the middle of the sanctum in Dema.
