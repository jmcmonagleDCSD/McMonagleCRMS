Lebron James



Lebron James is an american proffeisnal basketball player of the Nation Basketball Ascosiation "NBA". He has been in the league for 20 years and is considered one of the greatest of all-time. He has won 4 NBA Titles, 2 with the heat, 1 with the Caveliers, and 1 with the Lakers. He has also won 4 mvps, 4 finals MVP, and holds the all time scoring record for points in the NBA. He has also won 3 Olympic gold medals, and was the Olympic MVP this past olympics. While also being an all-time great, he's also an amazing public figure. He has donated millions of dollars twoards public school programs. His amazing charecteristics as well as his dominance on the court is what ultimetly lead to his sucsess.
