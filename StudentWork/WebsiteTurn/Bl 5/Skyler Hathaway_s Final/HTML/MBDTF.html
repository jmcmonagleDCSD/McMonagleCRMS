My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy

Album Ranking: 10/10

Important Info

This is Kanye's most criticaly aclaimed album. Winning a grammy for best rap album of the year and considered by many people to be his magnum opus. Nearly every song on this album is perfect and is an amazing album.

Important Songs

Runaway

This is Kanye's best song not my favorite song but it's his best. It is an amazing song that goes into why Kanye is what Kanye is and also features and amazing verse from Pusha T. I could go very in-depth on this song but I'll just leave it there

All of the Lights

This 1st Kanye song I ever heard and one of his most popular. He raps incredibly on this album and Rihaana's chorus is amazing here. He also get's Drake on this song not to have a verse but for backround vocals just at the end of the song

Dark Fantasy

This is Kanye's best intro from any album. It has an amazing sample and great lyrics and set's the tone of this album very, very well

    Top 5 Songs

  1. Runaway

Worst Song

So Appalled

This song is the only bad one on the album and when I say it's bad its bad. It is a very slow song and the beat is also really bad the song runs too long and the features don't add anything of substance to the album.
