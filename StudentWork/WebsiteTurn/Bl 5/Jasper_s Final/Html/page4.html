Walk up songs

Walk-up songs are energetic tracks played as athletes approach the field or stage, designed to hype both the player and the audience. These songs serve as a personal anthem, setting the tone for the performance or game ahead. For example, baseball players might choose classics like "Thunderstruck" by AC/DC or "Enter Sandman" by Metallica to energize the crowd. In the world of boxing, fighters often opt for high-energy songs like "Eye of the Tiger" by Survivor or "Can't Be Touched" by Roy Jones Jr. These selections create a unique atmosphere, building anticipation and excitement for the action to come.
