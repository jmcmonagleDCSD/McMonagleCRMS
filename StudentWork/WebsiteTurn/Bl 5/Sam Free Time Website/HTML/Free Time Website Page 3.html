Tournament

Tournament is where you can have 2-16 players/com fight each other in a one vs one, two vs two, three vs three, and four vs four. These battles can help you train and get better at the game. For example I could put all other players com and make their levels 1 that can help you train and unlock more moves.

Unlocking Powers

Every time you win a tournament you get a 20% chance that you will get a locked move. Each character has 50 locked moves 80 in total because of the 5 you start with like basic punch, you also can only equip 5. You can only unlock 5 moves using Brawl, you can unlock 10 moves in Tournament, 15 moves in Squad Fight, 20 moves in Sprits, and the bounus 25 from Master Mode. The reason why the numbers are like this because brawl is for begginers like level 1, Tournament is level 2, Squad Fight is level 3 and Sprits is level 4. There is a mode where that you can only do if you have all the moves called Master Mode.
