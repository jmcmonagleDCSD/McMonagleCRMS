Squad Fight is where the first two people fight then the winner of that round will face the other teams second fighter, and it repeats like that till you get depeat the other teams final fighter. It seems easy but it can be very hard if you don't know much about this game. That is why I made this website to help people get better by showing them how it's done. Maps should be used carfully in case you what to use a big or small map so make good choices when choosing a map.
