Brawl

Brawl is were you can make a public or private server or join a public or private server to fight together online. This helps players connect to each other and fight together. There are 5 modes of Brawl.

  1. Solo vs Com
  2. Solo vs Solo
  3. Team vs Team
  4. Team vs Solo
  5. Multifighter Mode

These are the gamemodes you can fight with. They help you have fun in this amazing game. If you or the person your fighting with wins theres a 20% chance a new character will show up and challenge the winner, this works in Solo vs Com(If you win), Solo vs Solo, Team vs Team(Who gets the most KOs wins), Team vs Solo(If the solo wins or the guy with the most KOs on the team wins), and Multifighter Mode(Whoever wins). There are tons of different maps to fight on. Like the haunted pumpkin patch map it turns to night and day it starts as day and not haunted then goes to night and there are ghosts.

Solo vs Com

Solo vs Com is where you fight the computer who will be either a random or certian freak depending on what you chose. You can chose who the computer is going to be or make it a random character you have earned or bought. Then you can change how hard or easy the com fights. This can be used to give yourself a challenge by putting it at max or you can use it to start small by putting it on min.

Solo vs Solo

Solo vs Solo is where a fighter fights one other fighter. There can be times where you can find a spot to hide and ambush the other player, but it will not be easy because the your map vision will shrink and grow depending on where the other player is. This can help and hurt in case you don't know where the other person is. Use this to help yourself and hurt the other player just make your choices wisely to beat the other player.

Team vs Team

Team vs Team is where everybody choses which team their're going to be on and how much people are on each team depending on the players choices. Then you can fight in an all out brawl where you can only attack the other teams players. With teams you can work on your team building skills and work together.
