Predators Of A Red Panda

For Red pandas to escape their predators red pandas will climb trees and run away from their predators like Snow leopards, and martens. Humans are their bigest threats because they are hunted for their fur and captured for the illegal pet trade. And we are also cutting down the forests they call home for lumber and paper.
