Effects

Effects can be added to anything. They can make a higher pitch or they can make a certain sound effect to add to a song. They can be very useful, especially to poppy songs.

Drum effects

Most drum effects are often used on the snare. They can be used to create a "smack' or a "pop" sound. Others are often used on the kick drum to make a higher/lower pitch sound effect. The cymbals often have sound effects to change the sound as well.

Guitar effects

Often, guitar effects include working with pitches and certain sounds. You can make a guitar sound a little different using effects like those of a synthesizer, just more limited in number.

Microphone effects

Microphone effects can make you sound a little different and can change your voice from a little robotic to a little smoother to a little higher or lower in pitch.
