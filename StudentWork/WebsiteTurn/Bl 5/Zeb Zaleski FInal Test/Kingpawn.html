How to defend against Kings Pawn Opening

To defend against Kings Pawn Opening go to C5 the pawn covering your right bishop.
Continue to bring pawns down the right side until you can
A is to Castle.
B is to bring your bishop is open on the king or queen.
To win the queen your pawn must defend you when you attack or else it is a free bishop,
and devolpes the queen. C is to eventually promote as he devolopes his castle, bishop, or a knight.

How to succsesfully attack with king's pawn opening

When attacking with king's pawn opening they are going to try the Sicilian Defense so you must move your knight to f3.
You can also move a pawn to d4.
You also can develop a knight or bishop.
Most people will to flank you on the right side to hopefully get a promotion and make your pawn a queen.
A good idea is to either out flank with a knight or open a rook and eat up the pawns if they are in a diagnol line like formation.
