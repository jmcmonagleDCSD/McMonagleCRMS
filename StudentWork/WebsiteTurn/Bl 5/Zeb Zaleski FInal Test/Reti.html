What is the Reti Opening?

The Reti Opening is starting out with the knight instead of a pawn.
This is my favourite opening because it has a lot of shock to it to the defense.
It is quite common but is very good.
Unlike Queen or King pawn opeing you already have a devolped knight.

How to Defend against the Reti Openings

The best defense against it is the Slav.
The Slav is where you from a triangle of pawns.
The Slav is bassically where if you take you get taken back.
It is super strong when some one has a major attack plan.

How to succsesfully use the Reti Opening

When using the Reti Opening the point is to develop a piece early on.
It is widely regarded for being the third best chess opening.
So when using it is key to open your kings pawn next allowing a bishop and queen to be developed.
