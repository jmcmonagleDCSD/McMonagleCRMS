Summary of the Games


Half Life

Black mesa 8:58 AM, You are scientist Gordan Freeman, Gordan gets to work late to start up the teleporter but since he was late the portal was messing with the power. You put this mysterious crystal from an unknown man in a suit and place it into the portal causing disaster across the facility and the entire world. Gordan finds a crowbar and fights through hords of aliens Zen to be specific then soon makes it to their "Leader", Once Gordan defeated the monster he was awoken by a mysterious man and trapped in an eternal void until he was free.


Half Life 2

City 17, 20 years later, Gordan Awakes from his eternal darkness and soon learns the world is now infested with the aliens and their is a new race of aliens currently enslaving the humans, Gordan reunites with his scientist friends from Black Mesa. Gordan is warned of the new alien race (The Combine) and soon has to fight them. Gordan goes on a journey to reunite with even more friends from black mesa while still fighting through aliens. Gordan learns that his friend Eli Vance was taken by the combine and ends up killing most of them off in the process. He defeats the core that sends most combine to the planet exploding the entire tower they were on. Eli,Kliener both escape City 17

Half life 2 Episode 1

Gordan awakes from the rubble and makes his grand escape from the now broken City 17 that soon explodes, Once he escapes a big explosion happens and his train crashes.

Half life 2 episode 2

Gordan gets up from the crash, him and alyx run through the forests fighting through even more combine. they reunite with everyone and fight the combine one last time before they send the missile towards their only portal to earth ending all the combines in the process.

Portal

Apeture Science Lab, Sometime around Half Life 2,You are Chell, You awaken from your slumber and figure out that your being tested on. A robot named Glados guides you with portals then soon gives you a portal gun to soon then figure out more puzzles, You learn the robot was just doing this for fun and wants to kill you now so then you escape. You fight the robot and succeed but get blown out of the facility then blacking out as you get dragged back in.

Portal 2

50,000 years after Portal 1, You awaken in a hotel room with no clue whats going on. as you go to exit the hotel room a robot tells you about the exact amount of years you've been in their. as you learn how to walk and all that stuff you go back to sleep only to be awaken to a now ruin version of your hotel. a robot named wheatly greets you and guides you to the now destroyed Glados, but after messing with the controls Glados awakens, Glados starts putting you through puzzels until wheatly thinks of a plan to escape but causing him to become the leader and sending glados into a potato. Wheatly decides hes had enough and sends you and glados down to the abandoned version of Apeture science as you learn about the creator you try to escape and start experimenting with fluids that can grant you powerful jumping, and speed. you and glados go in for one final fight with wheatly and grab his head then teleport to the moon with your portal gun. you get dragged out of the portal and left in space with wheatly, but then glados grabs you and brings you back. glados decides to set you free and you exit through a door out in a mysterious field.

Why?


I like these games because they all give mass amounts of nostalgia everytime I play them. When I first saw someone play Half Life 2 I knew I had to get it. luckily enough my dad had both games on his steam account so then I could play. Years later I saw that the entire Half life series was on sale and I begged my dad to get it for me. I've played every Half life game and beat all of them. (Besides Half Life Alyx, That game is hard.) I've also played both Portal games and even became a play tester for Deadlock, Team fortress 2 was a great game aswell! All of these Valve Games are amazing and I suggest playing some of them at some point. Valve hasnt made a game in awhile but now they seem to be coming back with Deadlock and possibly more!
