Valve's Story


How It was Founded

Gabe Newell had zero experience with gaming, he would always get lost when even trying to create a game. But when all hope was lost he remembered that he once ported Doom onto the Windows 96, He decided to create the company. With lots of dedication Half Life was created. He didnt have too much education on games but he hired people who did. He wanted to make a Doom like game and thought of the idea of a alien based shooter. When creating models he took lots of inpiration off of real life people. When he created gordan it was just some big old dude with a big beard, it didnt really fit in with the scientist aspect of Half Life. The face of gordan freeman took high inspiration from one of the people he hired. when in the car with the game writer he decided on the name for the protagonist. Gordan Freeman. They were scared at first but finally the game was out. and you know what? It was a huge sucess, bringing many players such as myself to play it. thus starting the more games valve would create.

It didnt seem Valve was gonna do much about this and let it wait. They updated it occasionaly but it seemed like a total flop, leaving the app with a 2.4 to this day. Before Half life alyx released, People believed that Valve is just a cash grabbing company and didnt care to really make any more games and just sit back as other game devs upload to steam. that is until Half life Alyx released, People went crazy about it since it was the first Vr game Valve has made. Even though valve has some flaws it still is a great company and in my opinion probably one of the best!

How The Games were created


Portal

When Valve was creating even more games. they came across the Narbacular Drop, a project from the DigiPen Institude of Technology. The Beginning idea for the game was just placing portals on flat surfaces to manever through levels, A valve employee saw this and hired the student who thought of the idea and started to create Portal.

Portal 2

after portal's big success, they decided to start Portal 2. They decided to create Portal 2 after many employees wanted to create more portal mechanics. they expanded the lore and added way more things to the game. about 8 people worked on Portal but Portal two had about 4 times as many! Sadly just as portal was halfway done, the designer Kim Swift left Valve for Airtight games. But soon after Valve kept going with portal and soon enough it was out!

Half Life 2

Just about six months after Half life, Gabe Newell wanted to redefine the FPS genre. "Why spend four years of your life building something that isn't innovative and is basically pointless? If Half-Life 2 isn't viewed as the best PC game of all time, it's going to completely bum out most of the guys on this team." Said Gabe Newell. Half life 2 was hyped up to the max with instead of just being in Black mesa now you are going across the world! But when all seemed fine, disaster struck. A hacker had downloaded the Source code of the game and showed it to the world. Half life 2 wasnt even close to finishing and it seemed like they were gonna give up. The hacker Gembe was soon arrested after Valve planned to arrest them anyways. many years later they decided to return to the development and now its in the state it is now, A masterpeice.
