The Ranks

There are 9 Ranks in the game

  • Unranked
  • Bronze
  • Silver
  • Gold
  • Platinum
  • Diamond
  • Champion
  • Grand Champion
  • Super Sonic Legend

Photo by and from https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Season_One_Ranks.png

Unranked

This is the worst rank possible, unranked means you have not played 10 games to achieve getting a rank.

Bronze

This is the "beginner rank" meaning that most starters get this rank.

Silver

This rank is basically people who don't play ranked or don't like it a lot, or who are okay at the game.

Gold

This is the most common rank people get and most good players have this rank.

Platinum

This is a fairly hard rank to get if your an okay player.

Diamond

This is the rank were you see players starting to hit air dribbles or flip resets.

Champion

This is were players are really good.

Grand Champion

This rank has some off the best players in the game.

Super Sonic Legend

This is the best rank in the game and one of the hardest to achieve.
