Geometry Dash: World



Geometry Dash World differs a lot unlike the other levels. Instead of retaining the original level menu, each level is embedded into two different worlds, which are called Dashlands and Toxic Factory. You also have the ability to play the top levels made by other people, but online features and creation of levels are not available. There are 10 main levels in this version of Geometry Dash.
