Geometry Dash




Geometry Dash, is a rhythm game released by developer Robert Topala, also known as RobTop. The game was released on August 13, 2013.

How to play Geometry Dash

The game sends you to a level with a colored background after launched by you. Certain music will be playing in the background included as the game is referred to a "rhythm game." The player commands the icon to circumvent obstacles such as spikes, blocks, sawblades, walls, or ceilings until the closure of the game.

The game consists of a variety of levels you may choose from.

A main feature of the game the game is online mode. A very intersting feature in the game is the online mode, where you have the ability to customize your own levels and upoad them for the community. You also have the ability to befriend other players and view other people's profiles.
