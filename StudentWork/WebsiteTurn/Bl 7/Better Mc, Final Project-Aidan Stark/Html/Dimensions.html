All About The Dimensions

The Nether

The Nether has a massive revamp and a lot more structures to explore like the Soul Forge or the Burning Arena. There are also a whole lot of biomes that you can find like a biome where you can find some Rose Quartz instead of normal Quartz. There are also a lot more mobs- For example there is a bear that hangs on the ceiling and jumps you when you are under it.

There are plenty of biomes to explore in this revamp of the End including a lot of new mobs that are mosty friendly but some are very aggresive.
