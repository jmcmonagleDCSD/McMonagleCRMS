information about dogs.

dogs have four legs,their hind legs have a small curve at the ankale. the knee is up neer the hip. their tail is atached to the hip. the back goes from the loin to the head. the head is where the noes,stop,eyes,lips,ears, and whiskers are locaded. under the head is the neck,where the throat is locaded. under the neck is the fore legs(or the front legs,) are locaded. there you can find the carpus(wrist) and the pastern(thumb.)
