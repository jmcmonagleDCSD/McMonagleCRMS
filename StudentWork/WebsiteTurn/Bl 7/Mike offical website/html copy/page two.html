Tommy Guerrero

The infamous street skateboarder Tommy Guerrero was born on September 9, 1966. In San Francisco, California. Guerrero is most famous for his street skating through out the 80s. Tommy was featured in the movies, "The search for Animal Chin" he was also in "Future primitive" and he was in "Ban This". But now after his years with Powell Peralta and the Bones Brigade. He is now in the music industry and he plays the electrixc guitar and bass! He makes a mix of genres like rock, hip hop, and funk, soul, and jazz! But when Tommy was with The bones brigade he was very good at street skating and doing the big San Fransico hills. He would have a very fast paced style of skating. He was so good that he got his own decks.
