Lance Mountain

Lance Mountain is a legendary figure in the skateboarding world, known not just for his incredible skills on the board but also for his influence on the culture surrounding the sport. Emerging from the vibrant California skate scene in the 1980s, he became a key member of the iconic Bones Brigade, where his innovative style and creativity set new standards for vert skating. Beyond his technical prowess, Lance is celebrated for his laid-back personality and deep connection to the skate community, often seen mentoring younger skaters and sharing his passion for the sport. His contributions extend into art and design, reflecting his multifaceted talents and enduring legacy in skateboarding history. In addition to his impressive career in competitions and street skating, Lance has been instrumental in promoting skateboarding through various media, including skate videos and documentaries. His collaborations with brands and artists have helped shape skate fashion and culture, making him a revered icon. As a pioneer of the “skate and create” ethos, he continues to inspire skaters to explore their creativity both on and off the board. Through his enduring spirit and dedication, Lance Mountain remains a beloved figure whose impact resonates throughout the skateboarding community today.
